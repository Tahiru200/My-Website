Ghana Association of Student Planners (GASP) - UDS Tamale Chapter

The Ghana Association of Student Planners (GASP) is a student-led organization that seeks to empower future planners for sustainable development. The UDS Tamale Chapter of GASP is one of the most active chapters in the country. We organize workshops, seminars, and conferences to help students learn more about urban planning and sustainable development. Our goal is to create a network of young professionals who are passionate about making a positive impact on their communities. If you are interested in joining GASP, please visit our membership page for more information.
